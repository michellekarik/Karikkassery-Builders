Who We Are

A Family Built
on Trust

Karikkassery Builders is a residential construction sole proprietorship based in Ernakulam, Kerala. Founded in 2000 by Antony Karikkassery, the company is today owned and led by his three sons — Benny, Vincent, and Jose (Joppan) Karikkassery.

Over 25 years, we have built 40+ premium residential homes in and around Kochi. We specialise in ready-to-move homes across the Ernakulam district, with every project personally managed by the owners from foundation to handover.
